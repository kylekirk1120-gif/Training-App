Install on iPhone
Add Train Together to your home screen
How to install — 2 options
Option 1 — Easiest (Netlify Drop)
Free, no account needed, takes 2 minutes
1
Download the app file
The training-app.html file was downloaded when you clicked the link from Claude. Find it in your Downloads folder.
2
Go to netlify.com/drop on your computer
Drag the training-app.html file onto the Netlify Drop page. It will give you a free URL instantly — something like "train-together-abc123.netlify.app"
3
Open that URL in Safari on your iPhone
Type or AirDrop the URL to your phone and open it in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome).
4
Tap the Share button
In Safari, tap the Share icon (box with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen.
5
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen". Name it "Train Together" and tap Add. Done — it now lives on your home screen like any other app.
Option 2 — GitHub Pages (also free)
If you have or want a GitHub account: create a new repository, upload the HTML file as index.html, go to Settings → Pages → enable from main branch. GitHub will give you a permanent URL at username.github.io/repo-name that you can add to both your and your dad's home screens.
Sharing with Dad
Once your URL is live, just text it to your dad. He opens it in Safari on his iPhone and follows the same "Add to Home Screen" steps. You'll both have the same app — use the You / Dad toggle inside to switch between profiles. Note: data saves locally on each phone separately, so use the comparison screen when you're together to compare progress.
